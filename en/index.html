Student Conference of Information, Functional and Cybersecurity (SCIFiC)

We are pleased to welcome you to the website of the conference organized by the Department of Computer Systems, Networks and Cybersecurity for students interested in cybersecurity. This is the foutrh conference where undergraduate and graduate students can present their research achievements.

Registration

WE INVITE YOU TO TAKE PART IN THE IV STUDENT SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL CONFERENCE "INFORMATION, FUNCTIONAL AND CYBERSECURITY" (SCIFIC)!

Abstracts and a request to attend the conference should be submitted using the Google form at, November 15, 2024.

Abstracts should be submitted electronically with the extension ".doc" or ".docx" and the title according to the template: Surname-Name-Section_(1,2)-Title_of_the_work. (Example: Yudin-Oles-Section_1-Security_IaC).
Organizational expenses are free of charge. The conference abstracts will be published in electronic form and will have the status of a scientific publication with an ISBN. However, if the participant wishes to receive a printed collection of theses, participant must contact with organizing committee. The cost of shipping the printed collection outside of Ukraine is discussed individually with the organizing committee.Payment details can be obtained through the organizing committee by writing to scific@csn.khai.edu.
Important! The participant must request printed theses at the same time as submitting the report abstracts.

Information about the conference

The conference will be conducted online. Conference working languages: Ukrainian, English.

Date of the conference: November 29 - 30 2024.

CONFERENCE TOPICS:
Section 1. Information and cybersecurity.
Section 2. Functional safety.

Sponsors and partners of the scientific conference: Kharkiv IT Cluster, Ukrainian Scientific and Educational IT Society .
Every year, valuable prizes are drawn among the best reports of the conference participants!
