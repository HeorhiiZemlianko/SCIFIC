Student Conference of Information, Functional and Cybersecurity (SCIFiC)

We are pleased to welcome you to the website of the conference organized by the Department of Computer Systems, Networks and Cybersecurity for students interested in cybersecurity. This is the third conference where undergraduate and graduate students can present their research achievements.

Registration

WE INVITE YOU TO TAKE PART IN THE III STUDENT SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL CONFERENCE "INFORMATION, FUNCTIONAL AND CYBERSECURITY" (SCIFIC)!

Abstracts and a request to attend the conference should be submitted using the Google form at , November 15, 2023.

Abstracts should be submitted electronically with the extension ".doc" or ".docx" and the title according to the template: Surname-Name-Section_(1,2)-Title_of_the_work. (Example: Yudin-Oles-Section_1-Security_IaC). Organizational expenses are free of charge. The conference abstracts will be published in electronic form and will have the status of a scientific publication with an ISBN. At the participant's initiative, the Organizing Committee may send the participant a printed version of the collection of abstracts. In this case, the participant must pay the Organizing Committee an amount of approximately 50 Euros for the printing of the abstract book. The registration fee is paid for one abstract. Payment details can be obtained from the Organizing Committee by writing to scific@csn.khai.edu.

Information about the conference

The conference will be conducted online. Conference working languages: Ukrainian, English.

Date of the conference: November 30 - December 1 2023.

CONFERENCE TOPICS:
Section 1. Information and cybersecurity.
Section 2. Functional safety.

Sponsors and partners of the scientific conference: CodeIT, Distributed Lab, Kharkiv IT Cluster, Ukrainian Scientific and Educational IT Society.
Every year, valuable prizes are drawn among the best reports of the conference participants!
